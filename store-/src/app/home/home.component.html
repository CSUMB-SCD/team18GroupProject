We love otters

The sea otter is a marine mammal native to the coasts of the northern and eastern North Pacific Ocean. Adult sea otters typically weigh between 14 and 45 kg, making them the heaviest members of the weasel family, but among the smallest marine mammals. Here at the clam store we aim to provide the very best products for otters around the world. 50% of online sales go to the conservation of sea otters!
